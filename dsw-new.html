Assigned Duties and Responsibilities
    The Dean of Student Welfare Is:
  • Advisor cum Treasurer of the University Student's Union.
  • Head of The Information Bureau in The University.
  • Coordinator, All Cultural Activities of The University.
  • Adviser to Foreign Students.
      • Conduction Of Youth Festival at:
    • Inter College Level
    • Inter District Level
    • State Inter University Level
    • Zone Level
    • National Level
    • International Level
  • Arrangements To Ensure Suitable Housing Facilities For Students.
  • Communication With The Guardians Of The Student's Concerning the Welfare of Students.
  • Arrangement of Travel Facilities For Students.

  • Anti Ragging Committee at University/UTD Level.
  • Student Grievances Redressal Cell.
  • University Proctorial Board.
  • UTD's Proctorial Board.
  • Physically Challenged Students Welfare Committee.
  • Economically Weak Students Welfare Committee.
  • Students Compensation Committee.
